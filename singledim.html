In the training session, in each trial, you will hear two sounds. Please identify whether the first or the second sound is breathier.

You will proceed to the test session when you correctly identify five pairs of sound in a roll.

Now you will begin the drag-and-rate task.

On the next six pages, you will see a sound icon. Click the icon to play the sound. You may listen to the sound as often as you like by clicking on the icon. Once you've listened to the sound at least once, move the icon into the box below by clicking and dragging the icon into the box. Once that icon is moved into a box, a new sound icon will appear above the box. Repeat the task above (listen to the icon, then move the icon into the box below) until all icons have been placed within the box

You should place the sound icon in the box from left (less breathy sounding) to right (more breathy sounding). Icons that sound equally breathy to you should be stacked one on top of the other within the box. You may use as much of the scale (from left-to-right) as you'd like

Once an icon is placed in the box, you can continue to listen to the sound by clicking on the icon, and can continue to move the icon around until you are satisfied with the placement of all icons. When finished, please click "Continue" to proceed to the next trial. There are six sets of sounds to sort and rate in total.
